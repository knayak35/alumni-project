About the SIS Alumni Platform

The SIS Alumni platform was created with a singular vision: to foster lasting connections between the generations of students who have walked through the halls of SIS schools. Our goal is to create a thriving, supportive network where alumni can reconnect, share their achievements, and offer guidance to the next wave of graduates.

Whether you’re just starting your professional journey or are an established leader in your field, SIS Alumni offers a space to stay engaged with your peers and give back to the community that helped shape you.

How we can Help You

Stay updated on alumni events, reunions, and online webinars. Engage in discussions, participate in virtual conferences, or attend regional alumni gatherings organized globally.
